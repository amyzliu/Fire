our first sparc: the inspiration

Making new friends is not easy, especially for students who are regularly on the move. Three first year students gathered to form a solution to the modern dilemma of frequent mobility and short-term relationships. sparc facilitates the natural social process by matching students with other students who will be geographically close during the same time period. Making friends has never been simpler.

minimalistic UI and complex algorithms

sparc is an entirely open-sourced, user-friendly platform. We have implemented Clarifai's Machine Learning API to visually compare and extract similarities between the photos of users.
